# R3 Chart Audit Playbook

This playbook combines the Alleva walkthrough transcripts, the screenshot evidence, and the clarified workflow notes into one ordered checklist.

## Combined Flow

1. Verify client overview episode metadata.
   Record client name, original admission date, original discharge date, and every level of care shown for the episode.
2. Verify the primary clinician assignment.
   Confirm the field is populated correctly in Client Overview.
3. Confirm the Intake Packet (Initial) is completed at admission.
   Verify completed status plus client and staff signatures.
4. Confirm Client Rights in House (Initial) is completed at admission.
5. Review Attendance Policy Consent.
   Exactly one Accept or Decline choice must be selected.
6. Review Assurance of Freedom of Choice.
   Exactly one Accept or Decline choice must be selected.
7. Review repeated release and consent field patterns.
   Accept/Decline prompts must have exactly one selection, "Other" requires written detail, and physician fields must be completed unless "No PCP" is checked.
8. Review the emergency contact release / ROI.
   Confirm disclosure selections, signatures, active dates, expiration, and annual renewal logic.
9. Review UDS / lab screenings.
   Confirm roughly weekly random testing evidence from the Lab tab dates.
10. Review medication list accuracy.
   If medications are present, confirm the list is complete and each entry is marked as Home instead of Prescribed.
11. If the medication list is empty, find compensating "no meds" evidence elsewhere in the chart.
12. Confirm the biopsychosocial assessment is completed and signed at admission.
13. Confirm Medical History and Physical compliance.
   Either H&P within the last 12 months or referral within 30 days, including evidence found in biopsychosocial or case management notes.
14. Confirm the Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale is present.
15. Confirm the BARC instrument is present.
16. Confirm the ASAM assessment is present.
17. Confirm the GAD scale is present.
18. Confirm the PHQ-9 is present.

## Evidence Standard

For every checklist item, capture:

- Document or form name
- Location or tab in the chart
- Completed or signed date
- Expiration or renewal date when applicable
- Reviewer notes describing what passed, failed, or needs follow-up

## Policy Definitions Still Needed

- What "at admission" means operationally
- When the primary clinician must be assigned
- The one-year ROI renewal anchor date
- The weekly random lab tolerance window and grace period
- The 30-day anchor date for referral timing
- Whether elevated scale results require documented clinical response or only instrument presence
